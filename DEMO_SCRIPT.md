# WhatsApp AI Receptionist Demo Script

Goal: present the MVP to a dental clinic owner in under 3 minutes.

## 1. Pain Point

"Clinics often lose patients because WhatsApp messages are answered too late, appointment requests are not captured in a structured way, and nobody has a clear follow-up list."

## 2. Start Here

Open the sales demo home page.

Click one scenario card:

- Pricing question: `/chat?scenario=pricing&lang=en`
- Appointment request: `/chat?scenario=appointment&lang=en`
- Emergency question: `/chat?scenario=emergency&lang=en`
- Complex question: `/chat?scenario=complex&lang=en`

For Spanish, use `lang=es`.

## 3. Run The Chat Scenario

Click **Send scenario** in the patient chat.

Point out:

- The receptionist replies instantly.
- FAQs are answered without staff intervention.
- Appointment intent starts a structured lead capture flow.
- Complex or uncertain questions are escalated instead of guessed.

For a lead capture demo, complete:

- Patient name
- Phone or WhatsApp
- Service needed
- Preferred date/time

## 4. Show The Dashboard

Open **Clinic owner dashboard**:

- English: `/dashboard?lang=en`
- Spanish: `/dashboard?lang=es`

Point out:

- New leads
- Appointment requests
- Hot leads
- Human follow-up needs
- Most common questions
- Lead detail view

Say: "This is the part that prevents leads from disappearing inside WhatsApp."

## 5. Close

"The MVP is not trying to replace your team. It answers routine questions instantly, captures appointment requests while the clinic is busy, and gives the team a clean follow-up queue."

## 6. Validation Questions

- How many WhatsApp messages does the clinic miss or answer late each week?
- Which questions are repeated most often?
- What information does the team need before calling a patient back?
- Who currently owns follow-up?
- Would a daily summary of leads and follow-ups be useful?
- What would make this demo credible enough to pilot with real patients?
- Which integration matters first: WhatsApp, calendar, Google Sheets, or dashboard?
